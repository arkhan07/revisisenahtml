The New Standard in Motorcycle Communication

Built on a revolutionary cellular network-based protocol, Wave Intercom redefines motorcycle group communication. It eliminates distance and device limitations, overcoming traditional intercom systems with seamless connectivity.

Now with the latest feature, Friends Wave, it takes riding communication to the next level—connecting almost anyone through uninterrupted group conversations like never before.

Key highlights of Wave Intercom include:

  • Geo-Based Intercom
  • Friends-Based Intercom
  • Live Location Display
    in Real time
  • Intelligent Wave-to-Mesh
    Conversion
  • Cross-Brand
    Compatibility

  • Geo-Based Intercom, Simplicity meets innovations

    The philosophy behind Geo-Based Intercom is effortless connectivity, mirroring the ease of use of Open Mesh Intercom on Sena Mesh Intercom devices.

    With Sena's Open Mesh, connecting with nearby riders is as simple as pressing the Mesh button. Wave Intercom takes this simplicity to the next level by leveraging cellular technology. Instead of just tapping the Mesh button to connect over Mesh Intercom, a double-tap on the Mesh button instantly connects you to any nearby riders in Wave Zone. The Wave Zone covers a 1-mile radius in North America and a 1.6-km radius in Europe.

    If you are not a Sena device user, launch the Wave Intercom app on your smartphone and tap the Geo Wave button on the app's home screen to join Geo-Based Intercom.

    Users on your Friends List will stay connected until the Wave Intercom session ends. However, participants who are not on your Friends List will be disconnected if they move beyond a certain distance from your location. They will be disconnected beyond a 2-mile radius in North America and a 3.2-km radius in Europe.

    Wave Intercom ensures the same seamless experience while breaking free from the limitations of traditional mesh networks. Whether you're riding with friends or encountering new riders within your Wave Zone, Geo-Based Intercom guarantees instant, effortless communication with the push of a button.

    Enjoy the familiarity of Mesh Intercom usability combined with the global reach and power of Wave Intercom.

  • Friends-Based Intercom, Now more private

    Wave Intercom lets you enjoy limitless communication simply by adding friends to your list. Once added, you'll stay connected with your riding friends—no matter how far apart you are.

    For even more convenience and privacy, Wave Intercom introduces its latest feature: Friends Wave. Want to talk only with your selected friends? Want to keep your conversations private from random users? With the new feature, you can create your own private intercom groups. Choose members from your Friends List, search by nickname, or select nearby users.

    Wave Intercom connects everyone with cellular network coverage, offering seamless voice communication. Once you're in a Friends Wave, you can stay connected while riding—regardless of distance or location.

    Enjoy continuous, secure connections with Friends Wave.

  • Live location display, Ride with confidence

    Live Location Display shows the real-time location of your friends on the map of the Wave Intercom app screen, helping you keep your group ride organized and safe.

    With the Live Location Display feature, your riding group stays seamlessly connected. No need to stop for fuel or delays—if one rider needs to fill up, they can easily rejoin the group by tracking the exact location of other riders on the app's map.

    Stay organized, informed and navigate with confidence.

  • Intelligent Wave-to-Mesh conversion, Stay connected anytime

    Intelligent Wave-to-Mesh conversion feature automatically switches your Wave Intercom to Mesh intercom when cellular network reception is limited or unavailable, ensuring a continuous and reliable connection for all riders in your group.

    With Intelligent Wave-to-Mesh Conversion, your group communication remains uninterrupted. If the cellular network becomes unavailable, the Wave server seamlessly switches all participants to the same Mesh Intercom channel within seconds. When the cellular network is restored, all participants are notified, making it easy to switch back to Wave Intercom for extended range and features.

    Stay connected effortlessly, no matter where your ride takes you.

  • Cross-Brand Compatibility, Connect with No Limits, Even Across Devices

    Wave Intercom redefines connectivity with its unparalleled cross-brand compatibility. Whether you're using a motorcycle-specific Bluetooth headset or any other brand, connecting is effortless.

    Simply download the Wave Intercom app on your smartphone, launch it, and tap the Wave button on the home screen to begin.

    Stay in touch with your group, no matter the devices you use.

How to Start Geo Wave

    • Open the Wave Intercom app.

    • Double-tap the Mesh Intercom button on your compatible Sena device to start Wave Intercom.

    • Note: If your device doesn't support Wave Intercom button control, tap the Geo Wave button in the app to start.

    • When the map appears, you can start talking to anyone using Wave Intercom.

Note: You can switch between Wave Intercom and Mesh Intercom by tapping the Jog Dial or the Center button once.

How to Start Friends Wave

    • Go to the Friends tab.

    • Tap the Create Friends Wave button.

    • Select the people you want to chat with.

    • Tap the Start button.
