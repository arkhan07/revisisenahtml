CONNECTIVITY ELEVATED WITH MESH 3.0

The 60S redefines Mesh communication with Mesh 3.0, offering unparalleled connection strength and sound quality. Designed with dual-version Mesh, it ensures backward compatibility with legacy Sena Mesh products, allowing riders to connect across multiple generations of Sena devices effortlessly.

Mesh 3.0 guarantees a stable, high-performing intercom experience for groups, even in the most challenging environments.

THE POWER OF WAVE INTERCOM

The 60S sets the new standard as the ultimate motorcycle headset, featuring full compatibility with Wave Intercom, the revolutionary cellular-based group communication system with no limits. As the highest-end Sena model supporting Wave, it offers unmatched benefits, including a simple button interface for effortless control and Intelligent Wave-to-Mesh conversion.

Combined with the Wave Intercom, the 60S ensures your group stays seamlessly connected, no matter the distance or terrain.

  • Effortless Multitasking and Flexible Communication with Dual-Core Power

    The 60S is powered by a dual-core CPU with parallel architecture, delivering unmatched multitasking performance. Effortlessly manage multiple device connections while enjoying flexible communication options such as Open Mesh, Group Mesh, Bluetooth Intercom, and the revolutionary Wave Intercom. The advanced dual-core structure also powers flawless audio multitasking, letting you enjoy your favorite music while participating in intercom conversations.

    With Open Mesh Intercom, instantly connect with a virtually unlimited number of riders across six channels, enabling spontaneous group communication without complicated setups. For more private or structured communication, Group Mesh Intercom lets you create a closed network with up to 24 participants, ensuring clear and secure conversations within your riding group.Additionally, the 60S features Bluetooth Intercom, perfect for connecting with riders who don’t have Mesh-enabled Sena devices.

    Whether you’re communicating with nearby riders or coordinating with a large group without limitation, the 60S ensures flawless performance, letting you focus on the ride.

  • Unparalleled Audio Quality with 2nd Generation Harman Kardon Speakers

    The 60S takes audio performance to the next level with its 2nd Generation Harman Kardon 40mm speakers, perfectly sized to fit all helmet types. These powerful, high-resolution speakers deliver a wider dynamic range and significantly enhanced sound quality compared to its predecessor, the 50S.

    Riders will experience richer audio, even at higher speeds, where wind and road noise typically interfere.

  • Enhanced Voice Commands

    Equipped with a next-generation voice command engine, the 60S revolutionizes hands-free control. This enhanced system not only expands the range of available voice commands compared to the 50S but also delivers faster, more accurate performance. Even in challenging noise conditions, the 60S ensures seamless operation, empowering you to manage intercom settings, adjust music, navigate, and more – all with just your voice, keeping your hands on the handlebars and your focus on the ride.

  • AI-Based Noise Cancellation

    The 60S raises the bar for communication clarity with its cutting-edge AI-powered ambient noise suppression and echo cancellation. This intelligent system adapts in real-time to your riding environment, minimizing wind and road noise for crystal-clear voice transmission.

    Whether you're on a quiet backroad or a noisy urban street, the 60S ensures industry-leading clarity for both intercom conversations and phone calls, letting you stay connected without distraction.

PROTECT YOUR HEARING
WITH ADVANCED ANC

Riders are exposed to high noise levels from wind and engine sounds. The noise levels exceed 105dB at a speed of 60MPH, posing a high risk of hearing damage within just five minutes. The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) Earbuds reduce ambient noise by up to 25dB, maintaining safe noise levels. Protect your hearing with the ANC Earbuds—even after a long ride. (Sold separately)

Note: The ANC function is designed to protect hearing from excessive noise during rides. As it reduces surrounding noise, please be extra cautious for safety while riding. We recommend using it in closed circuits. Please be sure to check local regulations before using it on public roads.

DUAL HELMET SETUP READY

To ensure consistent audio excellence, the 60S single package includes two complete helmet clamp kits, each featuring 2nd Generation Harman Kardon speakers and microphones to accommodate your multiple helmets. The package also includes a 3.5mm earplug adapter, giving you the option to connect your own earbuds for a more personalized audio experience.

Additionally, the optional Active Noise Cancellation(ANC) earbuds are available as separately sold accessories. These specialized earbuds reduce noise levels by approximately 25 dB, helping to protect your eardrums from long-term exposure to excessive noise while riding, ensuring a safer and more comfortable experience.

Whether you are wearing your street helmet for the road or your adventure helmet for the trail,
the 60S guarantees immersive, high-quality sound every time.

CUSTOMIZABLE STYLE
WITH INTERCHANGEABLE FACEPLATES

The 60S offers a personalized touch with interchangeable faceplates and jog dial covers, allowing you to match your headset to your unique style. The package includes four sets of faceplates and jog dial covers in different colors, giving you plenty of options right out of the box.

DYNAMIC ILLUMINATION WITH RIDEGLOW

The 60S features RideGlow, a customizable LED array that adds both functionality and style to your ride. The dynamically animated LEDs provide a visual display of the 60S's current status and user input with smooth, eye-catching animations. Beyond functionality, the RideGlow LEDs are fully customizable using the Sena Motorcycling App, allowing you to personalize the colors and patterns to match your helmet, motorcycle, or personal style.

With RideGlow, your 60S doesn't just perform – it shines.

PRACTICAL ILLUMINATION WITH INTEGRATED LED FLASHLIGHT

The 60S comes equipped with a small yet highly practical addition: an integrated LED flashlight. Positioned at the front of the device, this built-in light provides convenient illumination for motorcycle riders, whether you're checking a map, inspecting your gear, or making quick adjustments during a roadside stop. Compact and easy to use, the integrated flashlight adds an extra layer of functionality to the 60S, making it an indispensable companion for your rides.

UNMATCHED DURABILITY
WITH IPX7 WATERPROOFING

The 60S delivers industry-leading waterproof performance, built to withstand even the harshest weather conditions. With an IPX7 rating, the 60S remains fully operational even when submerged in water. In rigorous testing, the 60S was fully submerged in a fishbowl and continued to operate flawlessly for over 20 hours until the battery was completely drained.

Whether you're caught in a downpour or navigating wet terrain, the 60S is designed to keep you connected, no matter the conditions.

EFFORTLESS ATTACHMENT
WITH MAGNETIC MOUNT

The 60S features a convenient magnetic mount, allowing you to securely attach the main unit to the helmet clamp with ease. This innovative design ensures a strong and reliable connection while making installation and removal quick and hassle-free. The no-release-latch system of the 60S is especially handy when wearing gloves, allowing riders to quickly and securely remove the main unit without any hassle.

Whether you're swapping helmets or storing your device, the magnetic mount simplifies the process.

HASSLE-FREE OVER-THE-AIR UPDATES
WITH THE ALL-NEW SENA MOTORCYCLES APP

The all-new Sena Motorcycles App elevates the 60S experience by providing seamless Over-the-Air (OTA) updates and easy device configuration. Wirelessly download and install the latest firmware to keep your 60S equipped with the newest features, enhancements, and improvements.

In addition to updates, the app allows you to personalize your 60S settings, from intercom preferences to customizing RideGlow LED colors. With its user-friendly design, the app ensures you’re always in control and ready for every ride.
